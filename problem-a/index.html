Peter Parker

Peter Parker is a New Yorker from Queens who, after being bit by a radioactive spider,

gains the power of a spider. After inadvertently causing his uncle Ben Parker's death,

Peter learns a valuable lesson: With Great Power Comes Great Responsibility. He then

dons the mask as Spider-Man. He is portrayed by Tobey Maguire in the Raimi Trilogy,

Andrew Garfield in The Amazing Spider-Man, and Tom Holland in the Marvel Cinematic Universe.

I like him because:

  • He's a friendly guy who does the right thing.
  • He's a smart individual that is also responsible.
  • I sort of understand his struggle about balancing your work schedule with social life.
